Chicken Tikka Masala

This Indian Chicken Tikka Masala is easy to make but makes sure to pack a punch! This flavorful mild-medium curry will go down as a family favorite. Serve with naan bread and mango chutney!

Ingredients (4 Servings)

  • 1 can of canned tomatoes (~14.5 ounces)
  • 4 tbsp of plain yogurt
  • 2 cloves of garlic, roughly chopped
  • 1 piece ginger, coarsely chopped (1 inch)
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 chopped onion

Directions

  • Combine tomatoes, yogurt, garlic, and ginger in a blender and process until smooth
  • Heat up some oil in a large frying pan over medium heat and add onions. Fry the onions and stir constantly for 3-4 minutes, or until they're soft.
  • Stir in your curry paste and fry for 1 minute, stirring once or twice.
  • Add in your tomato mixture and chicken to the pan and mix. Season with salt and pepper then remove the pan from heat.
  • Mix water and flour together in a bowl and stir into the frying pan. Place the pan back on to heat and bring to a boil.
  • Stir constantly for 5 minutes.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until thickened (around 15 minutes).
  • Sprinkle and garnish with cilantro and enjoy!

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 252 calories; protein 26.2g; carbohydrates 13.8g; fat 10g; cholesterol 65.5mg; sodium 399.1mg
